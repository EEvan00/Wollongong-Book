About this book

As Aria follows the lighthouse keeper's final notes, she discovers a web of coded messages that mirror a string of cold cases from the 1980s. Each entry in the journal reveals new suspects, pulling her deeper into the storm-swept community that would do anything to protect its own.

Black Night explores loyalty, grief, and the stories we inherit. With sharp pacing and immersive Australian settings, it's a gripping read that keeps you turning pages long after midnight — just remember to keep a light on.
