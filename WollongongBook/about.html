Stories That Shape Us

From a seaside pop-up to Wollongong's favourite bookstore, we champion community, curiosity, and the power of books to connect us.

Our Story

Wollongong Bookstore began in 2014 as a weekend market stall dedicated to sharing diverse Australian voices. Today, our shelves still reflect that mission: to create a welcoming space where stories spark conversation and everyone feels at home.

We collaborate with local schools, host weekly book clubs, and partner with independent publishers to showcase new authors. Whether you're stepping in for a quick recommendation or staying for a latte in our café, we're here to help you find the perfect next read.

What We Believe In

Four guiding principles keep us grounded as we grow our book-loving community.

  • Community First

    We build relationships through local partnerships, reader events, and welcoming spaces for every generation.

  • Curated Discovery

    Our team handpicks each title, prioritising inclusive stories, emerging voices, and timeless favourites.

  • Warm Hospitality

    Great conversations start with good coffee. Stay a while in our in-store café and connect with fellow readers.

  • Sustainable Choice

    We champion eco-friendly suppliers, run book swaps, and encourage mindful reading habits.
